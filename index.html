    Who we are?

    Designated Contractors emerged in 2017 with its well seasoned management team who have indepth experience in the real estate industry, to be at the forefront of Africa’s real estate market place, to deliver commercial, residential and leisure properties across the continent.

    MISSION

    To be Africa’s leading real estate developer, providing eco friendly and unique living standards and concepts to our clients.

    VISION

    To set the standard and pace for all our related services in all arenas through hard work, ethical business practices, and philanthropic endeavors.
